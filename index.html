WiFi Configuration

To reconfigure WiFi, power on your device and follow these steps:

  1. Press and hold the reset button on the device for 5 seconds
  2. The LED will start blinking rapidly, indicating setup mode
  3. Connect to the WiFi network "SmartSocket-XXXXX"
  4. Open 192.168.4.1 in your browser
  5. Enter your new WiFi credentials
